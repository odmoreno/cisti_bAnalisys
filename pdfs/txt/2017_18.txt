Development of an information system audit in a data center: Implementation of web application to the management of audited elements
ABSTRACT
The information system audit developed in a data center it was based on the study of the domains of COBIT; for this reason the survey for each component it was developed with the goal of obtain the necessary information for audit. The analysis of this information, permited to establish the degree of compliance with international standards, and determine the maturity, risk levels of each of the components analyzed, in order to recommend action to minimize the impact that these may cause in the information. For the automation of the process, a web application it was developed considering the characterist of the standard, and type of audit.
