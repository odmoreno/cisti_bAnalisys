Using Gamification Practices as Pedagogical Support in a Non-Governmental Institution
ABSTRACT
Gamification emerges as a phenomenon that has expanded the development of experiences and research in the area of education. The objective of this article is to present the application of gamified practices, seeking the development of children and adolescents participating in pedagogical workshops offered by an NGO, after understanding that students needed motivation and engagement for better use in reading, writing, interpretation and mathematical logic in school reinforcement activities.
