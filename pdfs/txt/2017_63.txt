Influential factors in the visibility and quality of the websites of private hospitals
ABSTRACT
This research analyses the Web quality and Web visibility of private non-beneficial and, at the same time, non-concerted hospitals in Spain, since it is understood that good Web quality leads to health canters being more transparent by disseminating information. In addition, four variables have been defined: good accessibility, presence of Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) indicators, quality accreditations and availability of 2.0 tools; to study which of these factors have a direct influence on Web quality.
