The concerns of financial auditors with Information Security : 10 recommendations to consider
ABSTRACT
The present article contributes to the information security theme and how it should be one of the concerns for financial auditors, providing ten recommendations. Information security serves to protect the information coming from the systems and devices in which itâ€™s used, stored, and transmitted by ending up being a mandatory requirement to minimize the risks associated with an activity or business, to ensure compliance with the legal provisions or of regulatory nature.
