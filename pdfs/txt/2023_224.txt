New classroom technologies: : Case study of 3D printers in Design course
ABSTRACT
This paper describes and analyzes the case study using 3D printers as additional tools to teach virtual 3D modeling to design students. The use of new classroom technologies in higher education has enabled a better understanding of virtual 3d shapes modeled., as well as introducing manufacturing skills to students. It was also strategic to motivate students to come back to presential activities in the post pandemic period.
