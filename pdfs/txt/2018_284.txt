A mobile application for the recognition of banknote: Analternative in the processing of images in people with visual disability
ABSTRACT
This article presents the results of a research project, the current state of social inclusion of people with disabilities supported in ICTs and the development of technological support applications (visual disabilities), in which the main objective was to determine the main parameters for the design and development of an application for recognition of banknotes by image processing based on android technology aimed at people with some type of visual alteration. The methodology used focused on the visual impairment characteristics of the end users. The results obtained showed that the technology to identify tickets of the new family in Colombia, is easy to apply through software that has a Lector_apk and a Lector_100.apk, as a simple solution in the daily task of handling cash.
