A experiência do usuário e a comunicabilidade aplicadas na avaliação da rede social Linkedin
ABSTRACT
The growth and spread of the Internet in the last years, several middle of communication has been appearing and among them, they are the smartphones and applications. There are many types of mobile applications and among them, they are the applications of social nets. In this perspective, this paper aims to evaluate through an observation study the communicability and user experience of the application Linkedin. The results show that there is need for reconstruction of the meta-message of the application evaluated.
