Evaluation of the User Experience of a platform to support the creation of school newspapers
ABSTRACT
The consumption and production of information, namely news, has changed with the evolution of digital services. In the case of young people, their main access source is social networks, in which the information is not always quality. In this context, project TRUE - Trustworthy news and Related content for a Unified writing Environment emerged focused on developing a platform to support news writing in creating and managing school newspapers, which aims to encourage critical thinking in young people about the content they access and create. This article describes the evaluation process of the solution from the perspective of the quality of user experience, momentary and continuous, based on usability, to improve the platformâ€™s functionalities and implement strategies to encourage its use.
