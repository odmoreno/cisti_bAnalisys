Digital georoute of the Yamana sector, Loja-Ecuador
ABSTRACT
This research is aimed at highlighting the geotourism potential of Yamana in the province of Loja for its promotion through digital tools. The area presents various points of interest from which 4 georoutes have been established that have been represented through tools such as Google Earth and Mapillary and, thus, tourists can access and understand the geological wealth of the sector.
