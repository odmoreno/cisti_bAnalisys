Need to formulate standards for using of digital tools in virtual campuses : A Study Experiment
ABSTRACT
This paper analyzes a case study of a survey carried out in a teacher training course (172) in which a set of digital tools is recommended, by said teacher, as instruments for improving online teaching. The diversity of these teaching tools in number and categories leads us to considerer the need to formulate guides in the sense of standards which help in the choice of said digital material and efficiently structure this type of teaching on campus.
