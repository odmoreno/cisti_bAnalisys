Proposal for a virtualized data center migration model for private cloud model for the new Brazilian army information technology pole
ABSTRACT
Cloud computing is no longer a myth for organizations, on the contrary. With the creation of the Brazilian Army Information Technology Pole, they also understands that migration to a cloud environment can help to improve the provision of services to its internal customers, modernizing its infrastructure. There are several solutions on the market, proprietary and open source, offering the possibility to create a private cloud. But the main question is what to do with the systems that are in traditional environment? So this article aims to proposing a migration model of their systems that are hosted on a traditional model for a private cloud environment.
