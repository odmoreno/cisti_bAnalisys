Paper title in EnglisProposal for the patterns definition based on good practices for the electronic government systems development
ABSTRACT
The purpose of this work is to analyze the feasibility and usefulness of developing a method to define e-Government Good Practices patterns that can be applied in the Conceptual Model of the systems of the public sector in order to facilitate its construction with greater quality level. The dimension that is tried to optimize with this proposal is the Electronic Government systems quality level of Argentina in particular, and of Ibero-America in general, since a successful solution model has not been identified to take as a starting point. This article explains why we can use patterns to represent e-Gob Best Practices and are put to consideration Objectives and Methodology of a research that results in a method to define and implement these patterns in the Conceptual Modeling activity of said software.
