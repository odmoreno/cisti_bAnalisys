Computer system for the management and scientific divulgation of the “Universidad Nacional de Loja”
ABSTRACT
This article presents the computer system for the management and publication of scientific production (SIPAC), which provides information to members of higher education institutions in Ecuador (“Universidad Nacional de Loja”), with the management and publication of scientific production in the different areas of knowledge. SIPAC allows researchers to visualize scientific content, create and identify related research groups, dissemination of the results of research projects, among others. The SIPAC has been developed considering the requirements that the “Consejo de Educación Acreditación, y Aseguramiento de la Calidad de la Educación Superior (CEAACES)”, requests in the accreditation processes of higher education in Ecuador.
