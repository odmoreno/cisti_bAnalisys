Continuous audit. A new paradigm of audit?
ABSTRACT
Continuous auditing is considered by many authors as the new paradigm of the audit, hence the need to understand its basic concepts, the main guidelines proposed by international organizations grouping accountants and auditors and the need and importance of this technology represents for modern auditing in an organizational environment dominated by information and communications technologies. This is one of the objectives of this document provides a basis for understanding the positions of various authors on the consideration of continuous auditing as a new paradigm audit.
