Interactive Television and Second-Screen Devices: Presentation Strategies for Notifications
ABSTRACT
The present document reports the main concepts that create the structure for the relation of the ecosystem television - content - second-screen. As the final goal is to develop a mobile app for second-screen, inserted in the Smartly project, that integrates a notifications' scheme for the user, it was necessary to carry out a research of the existing mobile apps in the market, colecting data about the notifications layout and its prototyping processes. The main focus of this work consists, therefore, in creating a set of guidelines for the development of the app and its respective notifications with the purpose of understanding which are, in reality, the notification characteristics in second screen that influence their receptivity.
