Proposal for basic contents the subjects of Database in graduate studies in computer science at the Spanish universities adapted to the EHEA
ABSTRACT
This communication is framed within a broader research study referred to the basic concepts that students should know when considering first-time Database in Computing Degrees in Spanish universities. In this work, we have submitted our findings we obtained in pretrial community education experts work mainly working in the area of databases, so that we can conclude what the most important basic minimum content that students should know when studying the subject for the first time Database in Computing Degrees in different specializations. To carry out the work we used a questionnaire as a tool. Which was sent to various experts, specifically selected for this purpose, so that, depending on your answers, we could perform a basic proposal contained in one or more subjects Database.
