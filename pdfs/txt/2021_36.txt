Teaching and Learning of Interface Design: An Experience Using Project-Based Learning Approach
ABSTRACT
This paper presents an experience report on the use of the Project Based Learning approach with the evaluation and design of interfaces with a focus on teaching and learning the contents of the Human-Computer Interaction discipline of the undergraduate course in Software Engineering from University of Bras√≠lia in the second semester of 2019. The methodology used was participatory research, of an exploratory nature, with a qualitative approach and with data collection technique through a questionnaire. The research participants were 63 undergraduate students and an instructor. The results of this research reveal motivation and engagement of students by the Human-Computer Interaction discipline in a Software Engineering Course, in addition to the approval of the use of this approach by the students and the professor involved. .
