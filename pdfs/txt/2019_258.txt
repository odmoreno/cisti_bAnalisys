Virtual Tutoring System with Interactive Feedback between Teacher-Student
ABSTRACT
This document presents a proposal for student support through a platform that has the purpose of facilitating interaction between a student and his or her teacher, called the UTPL Student Feedback Platform. It consists of a tool that functions as a forum: the teacher places the name of the topic to be discussed and begins by contributing main ideas of the topic, the students contribute with information of the topic considering that their participation is qualified. Currently there are no solutions related to the proposed proposal, or at least with characteristics similar to those it has. In order to realize the proposal, the use of client-server architecture with a cascade methodological approach, respecting each phase of development in order to obtain a quality product.
