Assessing the benefit of ICT for the development of skills in microeconomics: a case study
ABSTRACT
The objective of the research is to quantitatively determine the academic benefit obtained by the students of the Open and Distance Modality of the Private Technical University of Loja, enrolled in the subject of principles of microeconomics during six consecutive academic periods and who participated in the project. university connectivity to the virtual learning environment (EVA). To achieve the objective, the technique of Propensity Score Matching (PSM) was applied, maintaining the control groups based on the results obtained, it can be determined that there are differences statistically significant in the academic performance of the students who applied the technique and the control groups, but this does not allow us to conclude that there is an important qualitative change in terms of academic performance since the absolute value of the mean differences are not significant.
