Revisiting influencing factors in choosing accounting as a profession
ABSTRACT
This study aims to identify and analyze factors influencing the choice of accounting for a future profession. Based on a representative sample (N=383) of undergraduate students of socioeconomic sciences at the University of Minho, this work presents results on the influence of social groups, expectations associated with the profession, personal background, as well as stereotypes of accountants and the accounting profession, for choosing to study accounting.
