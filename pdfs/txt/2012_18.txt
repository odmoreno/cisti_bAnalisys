Game engine for creating e-learning assessments
ABSTRACT
Over time, methods of evaluation in the education process has been exhausting and not very attractive for students. Currently there are different ways of assess knowledge through activities that promote the interests of the learners, so they find entertainment in these processes. This article describes the implementation of Game Engine project, which involves the creation of assessments through playful elements within a virtual learning environment.
