Conexão Vida: A Proposal to Assist in the Treatment and Communication Between Patients and IMIP Medical Staff
ABSTRACT
Cancer treatments have evolved in recent decades, with new methods, drugs and technological advances. However, the success of treatment is related to other factors other than medicine itself, such as: regular medical appointments, exams and communication with a multidisciplinary team. In this scenario, the proposal for a multiplatform system called Conexão Vida, which is a permanent communication tool in patients and caregivers with a multidisciplinary team of the IMIP Hospital, has arisen. Using technological techniques of the market, the communication program that aims to solve the processes without communication process, with a focus on improving the quality of life of the patient and family, thus providing an empowerment of the same regarding its treatment and the limitations of its use.
