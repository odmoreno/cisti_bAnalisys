Information Technology Artifact in the form of Responsive Web Design for Smartphones : Mobile Compatibility Test on SMEs Websites
ABSTRACT
The purpose of this article is to carry out a compatibility test on 30 Portuguese restaurant websites to determine if such websites have a responsive design. The methodology used is descriptive-quantitative. The test was carried out on Google's free Mobile Friendly Test platform, which certifies that a website is compatible with Smartphones. The compatibility test revealed that 57% of the websites of the surveyed SMEs are compatible with Smartphones.SMEs can apply this compatibility test on their websites to confirm that they have a Responsive Web Design, which fits the Smartphone screen. So that this site attracts and retains new customers coming from the web.
