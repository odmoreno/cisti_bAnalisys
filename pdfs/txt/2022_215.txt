Information System as support in the care of children with congenital heart disease
ABSTRACT
This article presents the description of the system developed for a foundation in the city of Tijuana, Baja California, Mexico, dedicated to caring for children with congenital heart malformations. The objective of this article is to present the methodology chosen for project management according to the conditions of the work team, the needs of the project owner (product methodology. This article also describes the phases in the design and the description of the products to be developed. owner) and based on the identified requirements. It is determined to work with an agile SCRUM methodology. The phases in the design and the description of the products to be developed are described. Evidence of the development of a support system for the administration of the Baby Cardio Foundation, which benefits the community of Baja California, is shown.
