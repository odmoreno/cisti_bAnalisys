Benefits of ERP systems implementation in business in Brazil: Case studies
ABSTRACT
This research presents impacts of benefits of the ERP systems (Enterprise Resource Planning) implementations in two companies in Pernambuco, Brazil. It was carried out two case studies and an exploratory research, interviewed agents of Supply, Making, Sales and Distribution, Finances, Accounting and Information Technology (IT)), applied questionnaires for agents, end-users and employers. The information aimed that the motivations for adoption of ERP systems screw needs of organizational control, processes standardize, management and to attend Brazilians legislations. Business better practices and cost-benefit were of moderate meanings. This study show it were important the benefits and the contributions for the business growth in the companies.
