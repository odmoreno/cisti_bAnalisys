Web application for support tutoring system
ABSTRACT
This paper describes the process of creating a Web application to support the tutoring system of a higher education institution. Application that allows the registration of interaction sessions between tutors and tutored, as well as the fill of questionnaires, both by the Students and by the Teachers Tutors. All this information helps the Student Provider to make decisions and define interventions, as well as in the preparation of the annual report to be presented to the academic direction. After deploying this application the mentoring program had an exponential increase the level of participation/membership, both by tutors and by tutored.
