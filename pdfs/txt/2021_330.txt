Digital Influencers Role in the XXI Century
ABSTRACT
The last decade has seen a lightning change in the way the marketplace moves. Traditional marketing channels are increasingly becoming obsolete, giving way to more appealing digital platforms that attract the public's attention. Digital influencers play a key role in social media for brands to achieve their main goal, which is to reach a high number of consumers. Thus, the purpose of this study is to highlight the attributes of influencers that consumers value most, favoring the purchase of a particular brand over another.
