Hospitals and Web 2.0: Potentialities in social media
ABSTRACT
Social Web has caused a revolution for communication in the organisations. Hospitals, like complex and organised entities, have not been an exception. The digital context allows increase the communication channels with the citizens and media. For hospitals, it represents a double opportunity: a) for institutional communication or corporate reputation and b) for health communication, in their role of educating health agents.
