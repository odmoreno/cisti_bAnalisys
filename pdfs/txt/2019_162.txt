A Preparation Game in Education on an Arcade Platform: an Attractive Possibility
ABSTRACT
This paper presents the process of usability of a game that simulates the resolution of questions of the ENEM (Exame National do Ensino MÃ©dio). So, with this possible, in an attractive way to students, another possibility of exercising their knowledge throughout their preparation for the exam. In order to achieve this objective, a qualitative research was developed, which demonstrates, in its results, the interest of students in using a platform like the one created in this project. In addition to the research with students, studies were made in the field of education technology, investigating the impacts of this approach in learning. The game was developed to run on an arcade machine in a way that is visually more attractive. For the production of the software and the arcade machine, specific tools such as NetBeans, Java and MySQL Server were used. After the initial tests of the arcade with students in a campus, it was possible to observe its positive impacts about the attractivity.
