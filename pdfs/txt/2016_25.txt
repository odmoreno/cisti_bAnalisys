Proposed methodology for learning english with the use of TICs: Case: Universidad tecnológica equinoccial
ABSTRACT
The study presents a proposal for Learning English as a Foreign Language at the “Universidad Tecnológica Equinoccial” (UTE), making use of the Information Technology and based on a previous pedagogical and technological analysis. Internal diagnostics assessed the use of virtual platforms used at UTE and deepened with a diagnostic field research, interviews with some teachers and students, a total of 3072 students and 27 teachers. From the results, a methodological proposal that presents the tools used, components, evaluation, a class structure and practices to be applied to improve Learning English is made. The methodology emphasizes the oral production and listening, through permanent Practices and Activities.
