Strategies for sustainable regeneration of tourist settlements on the Mediterranean coast. Applying ICT to maps and architectural projects in the context of R & D
ABSTRACT
The paper presents how the TIC has been employed in the redraw process of the cartographic documents in the research project I+D+i "Strategies for Sustainable Regeneration of Tourism Settlements on the Mediterranean coast " carried out by members of the IAM of the School of Architecture La Salle URL. This project aims to establish urban and architectural guidelines for a sustainable regeneration of coastal towns in the Mediterranean. This paper presents the first phases, which consists in drawing some maps of the territory, in different scales of representation, on which we can detect the different objects of study, then to analyze their problematics, in the way to propose the regeneration project for the selected buildings and his environment.
