Agency of the Future and the New Role of the Strategic Planner: New Planners for Digital Era
ABSTRACT
This work focuses on the figure of the Account Planner in the current communication agency and its potential to pivot the communication agency of the future. Objectives: to identify the new needs of customers and find out the role of the account planner in the agency of the future. We used a quantitative methodology, survey, from a database composed of 500 professionals of strategic planning belonging to national and multinational advertising agencies. Results point out a new role of the account planner, as a professional with strategic and creative thinking and skills, with a high degree of understanding of the client's needs. It must be multidisciplinary, rather than specialized, oriented to results and with basic knowledge of community manager, big data and evaluation of networks, to provide comprehensive knowledge to specialized experts.
