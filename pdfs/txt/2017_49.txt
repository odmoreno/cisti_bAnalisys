Design of digital games in the initial training of Biology teachers from Brazil and Portugal: A research proposal
ABSTRACT
This research is being developed in the context of a doctoral project in Educational Sciences, specialty of Educational Technology, at the University of Minho, Portugal. The investigation of the role of Digital Games Design (DJD) in the initial training courses of Biology teachers in Brazil and Portugal is aimed. As a methodology, Design Based Research is developed through strategies such as documentary research in the university curricula, interview and formative interventions, aiming at the use of the DJD as a learning tool, from the perspective of a transformative and constructionist education.
