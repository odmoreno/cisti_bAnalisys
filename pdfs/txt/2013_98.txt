ABSTRACT
Today there is a huge growth and development of local networks and Internet, so it becomes possible to develop applications targeted for WEB as numerous benefits, some of them passing by easy accessibility and navigability. This application is the creation of a prototype system to assist diagnosis of Alzheimer's disease and its main objective to provide a rapid diagnosis through existing medical examinations. Through the developed application can access a wide range of disease-related information, talk to a doctor, do online exams, making management of users and insert new medical tests as they arise.
