Calidad de las web de los hospitales privados web quality of private hospitals
ABSTRACT
The objective of the study is to develop a descriptive analysis to assess the quality and characteristics of the websites of private hospitals with non-charitable character in Catalonia. It questions whether the accessibility, usability, interactivity, updating content, levels of quality, privacy, web 2.0. and the information submitted in these private hospitals on their websites are acceptable or, on the contrary, no.
