From mathematical model of communication to the communication model of virtual universe
ABSTRACT
The new technological interfaces brought facility of access to communication by the increase of storage capacity of news and by the processing speed of information in real time. Thereby, the global interaction based on the sharing of information and knowledge, and advances in communication technologies, have changed the concept of communication in the society. Here, we present transitions about traditional communication models until communication model of virtual universe. The research is qualitative and empirical descriptive, and was developed in June to December 2016 in Department of Statistics and Information of the Federal Rural University of Pernambuco, in Brazil.
