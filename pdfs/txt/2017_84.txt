From information capitalism to the media capitalism the importance of the broadband in a global world
ABSTRACT
Nowadays, we are entering a new phase: The media capitalism where the market goes beyond new borders through the use of the internet, becoming new factors of economic development for the countries. The media-capitalism is closely linked to the cultural sector and the to the communication technologies. There is one of the biggest platforms for growth in the international markets. In addition, we do believe that the dynamism of hi-tech largely depends on the attractiveness, creativity and accessibility of the content towards the consumers.
