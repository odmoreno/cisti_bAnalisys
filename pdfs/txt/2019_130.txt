Review of Literature with Support of webQDA Software
ABSTRACT
The literature review is a process that can be optimized with the support of digital tools. The primary objective of this research was to identify the functionalities of the webQDA software according to the stages of the Literature Review. Literature Review is considered as a qualitative approach, so it is fundamental to know the potentialities and limits of digital tools available in the market. It is a Research & Development project, which presents the implementation of new functionalities in webQDA software through iterative and incremental processes. The results demonstrate that the Literature Review studies, in this specific case, carried out by the multidisciplinary team of webQDA software, allowed the decision making in the improvement of this software and implementation of new functionalities in order to satisfy the needs of the users.
